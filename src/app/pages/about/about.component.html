ABOUT US

Your Project Is In Good Hands

Middleman Construction Company has been serving the San Antonio commercial construction community since 1998. We specialize in unique projects such as commercial retail and office spaces, mixed-use buildings, multi-story construction, medical center layouts, and advanced interior finish-outs.

Through extensive planning, scheduling compliance, and open-book organization, our team delivers results on time and under budget. We work closely with architects and designers to incorporate designs and layout formats that satisfy both client satisfaction and budgetary guidelines.

Middleman has adopted an extensive subcontractor qualification process to ensure that all on-site trades provide quality and are held to the upmost accountability. This dedication has made us an award-winning builder honored by the Associated Builders and Contractors (ABC), American Subcontractors Association (ASA), and the U.S. Green Building Council.
